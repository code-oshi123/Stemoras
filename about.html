How it began

A quiet craft, a lasting bloom.

Stemora’s began at a kitchen table in 2021. Our founder, Clara, had always been passionate about craft — and when she discovered that pipe cleaners could be coaxed into gorgeous, detailed flowers, she couldn't stop making them.

What started as handmade gifts for friends and family quickly grew into something more. People kept asking: "Where did you get that flower?" The answer was always the same — she made it.

Today, Stemora’s ships to customers across the country. Every single flower is still made by hand, with the same care and attention that went into that very first bloom on that kitchen table.
