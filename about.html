Our Beginning

Where Every Petal
Tells a Story

"What started as a simple hobby in Gampaha blossomed into something truly special."

Stemora’s was founded in 2026 in Gampaha, Sri Lanka by Kusum Perera — a passionate crafter who has always loved creating handmade art, especially pipe cleaner flowers. Her journey began with a single hand-twisted rose.

Each flower is carefully designed and handcrafted to bring beauty that never fades. No two pieces are exactly alike — every bloom carries the warmth and personality of the hands that made it.

Stemora’s was created to share these unique flowers with people who seek meaningful gifts and lasting decorations — made with patience, love, and creativity.
