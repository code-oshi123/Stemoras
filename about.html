Bloomy Twists in Sri Lanka

Crafted from a small dream in Gampaha.

Bloomy Twists was founded in 2026 in Gampaha, Sri Lanka. What began as a simple hobby quickly grew into a cosy handmade flower shop.

Our founder, Kusum Perera, has always loved creating handmade crafts, especially pipe cleaner flowers. Each new bloom started as a curious experiment — twisting, shaping and layering soft stems until they looked like real petals.

As friends, neighbours and family started requesting her creations for birthdays, weddings and home decor, Bloomy Twists slowly turned into a small business filled with colour and heart.

Every flower is carefully designed and handcrafted to bring beauty that never fades. Bloomy Twists exists to share these unique handmade flowers with people who want meaningful gifts and decorations that feel personal and lasting.

The hearts behind Bloomy Twists

Meet the makers

Kusum Perera

Founder · Bloomy Twists

✿

Kusum Perera is the creative heart behind Bloomy Twists. She designs and handcrafts each pipe cleaner flower bouquet with patience and creativity. Her love for handmade crafts turned a simple hobby into a colourful flower shop.

Every bouquet carries a little piece of her story — hours of careful twisting, shaping and arranging until each flower feels just right.

Ushani Perera

Co‑Founder · Digital Studio

❀

Ushani Perera manages the digital side of Bloomy Twists. She developed the website, looks after marketing, and connects with customers online.

Her goal is to gently bridge handmade craft with modern online shopping, so more people can discover the colourful bouquets created in their Sri Lankan studio.
